Some Useful Cookery Tips...

Put a damp paper towel under your cutting board. This prevents it from swiveling around while you're trying to do something with a very sharp knife.

Add a little salt to everything. Yes, everything.It helps to bring out, accentuate, and deepen all sorts of flavors and will help your sweet treats taste more complex.

Pull cookies out before they're done. It's always better to undercook than overcook. And cookies will continue to cook on the tray after you take them out of the oven.
